Welcome to Bridge Builder!

Jason Shatz(Founder, Director of Business Development)

J.D. Shatz founded Bridgebuilder in 2016 after searching, to no avail, for initiatives that would address America’s crumbling infrastructure. Having grown up in New Jersey, he experienced the effects firsthand and decided to do something about it.

Shatz was previously a writer and researcher in various forums, and he still writes from time to time. He is also preoccupied with learning the code that will help bring his many ideas to life.

Currently studying web development at the New York Code and Design Academy, he holds a B.A. in Government and Writing from Wesleyan University.

Gino Cortes(Director of Human Resources)

Gino grew up in Jackson, NJ. Immediately Gino found himself wanting to learn as much as he could, including the math and sciences. Now Gino is eager to learn as much as he can about technology, infrastructure and how to improve America’s passageways. He is currently studying web development at the New York Code + Design Academy.

James Hwang (Director of Global Operation)

James saw an opportunity in rebuilding America's infrastructures with Bridgebuilder, which he strongly believed in their mission of helping others. Having a diverse background as a minority in United States, he now strives to expand and spread the vision of Bridguebuilder globally.

James was born in South Korea but spent time in Texas during his middle and high school years. He moved to New York for his college, Stony Brook, which he obtained a BA in economics. He was previously affiliated with digital advertising and Korean government agenices, but now he is attending NYCDA to develop his coding skills.
